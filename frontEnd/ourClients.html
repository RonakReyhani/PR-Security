Standard Bar

Providing a safe environment for restaurant and bar patrons and employees was a necessity for The Standard directives in these times. They wanted to Secure their establishment with a professional team who are highly-trained, experienced, courteous and dependable Security Officers. Based on our expertise, our Services assess, customize, develop and enforce the most ideal and cost-effective security procedures. These procedures are designed to maximize the efficiency and effectiveness of their venue’s security policies and are tailored uniquely for their premises. PR Security Duties for "The Standard" Include:

  • Greet patrons/employees/vendors
  • Access Control for patrons/employees/vendors
  • Deter & eliminate vulnerabilities/immediate danger
  • Pro-actively alert potential issues to Management
  • Report all illegal activities & hazards to Management
  • Assist the Venue with all VCGLR conditions on the liquor licence.
